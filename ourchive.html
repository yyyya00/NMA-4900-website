Ourchive

Ourchive explores memory as something stored, translated, and reactivated through systems. The exhibition moves between personal traces and digital structures, asking how archives are shaped by screens, interfaces, transitions, and acts of retrieval.

Interphases

Interphases focuses on states of transition, internal shifts, and in-between conditions. This floor can hold works about memory, emotion, atmosphere, latency, and becoming—spaces where experience feels less fixed and more fluid, layered, and unstable.

[ FLOOR X ]

Interfaces

Interfaces centers on contact zones: screens, systems, surfaces, and points of exchange. This floor can frame works around mediation, visibility, access, control, and technological form— where memory becomes navigable, clickable, translated, and displayed.
